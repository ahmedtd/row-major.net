Note: Images from project 5-13 all show color bleeding and environmental illumination. This is because I use very deep Monte-Carlo raytracer that accurately gathers spectral power information from throughout the scene. So, for example, the caustics formed by reflective and refractive objects are themselves sources of illumination that can cause color bleeding and shadows.
